Pulled Pork Sandwich

click here for more recipes


Ingredients Instructions
Pork butt (8-12lbs) and Weber KC BBQ Rub Generously apply the rub all around the pork. After this, put it on the smoker for hours at 180 degrees Fahrenheit for 7 hours.
After this, wrap the pork butt in tinfoil and put in then over at 225 degrees Fahrenheit. Check temp in pork with meat thermometer until lowest temp is 203 degrees Fahrenheit.
Once the desired temp is reached, take out of oven and let rest in closed tinfoil wrapping for 45 minutes.

BBQ Baby Back Ribs

Click here for more recipes


Ingredients Instructions
Two or three full racks of ribs and Weber KC BBQ Rub. Cover the ribs entirely in the rib rub and then put on the grill at 180 degrees Fahrenheit for 6 hours.
Put apple or cranberry juice into a spray bottle. Spray the top and sides of the ribs with the juice every 45 minutes during the initial 6 hours.
After 6 hours, turn the grill up to 225 degrees fahrenheit for 4 hours, or until the meat is tender enough and the bones are beginning to show on the sides.

Beef Brisket Sandwich

Click here for more recipes


Ingredients Instructions
1 12-15lb whole brisket and Weber KC BBQ Rub Cover the brisket with the rub. Next, put it on the grill at 180 degrees Fahrenheit for 4 hours.
After 4 hours, turn the grill up to 225 degrees Fahrenheit for 3 hours.
Put apple or cranberry juice into a spray bottle. Spray the top and sides of the brisket with the juice every 45 minutes during the time the brisket is on the grill.
Tin foil After the 3 hours at 225 degrees Fahrenheit, take the brisket off the grill and wrap it in tinfoil. Next, put it in the over. Periodically check the temp of the meat until the lowest recorded temp is 203 degrees Fahrenheit.
Once the desired temp is reached, take the brisket out of the oven and let rest, covered, for 45 minutes.
Lastly, cut and serve on pretzel buns for most enjoymeny.
